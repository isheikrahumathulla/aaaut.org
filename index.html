A Legacy Rooted in Excellence

Established in 2009 as a constituent college of Anna University, Chennai, the University V.O.C. College of Engineering, Thoothukudi has grown into a centre of quality technical education for the southern districts of Tamil Nadu. The Alumni Association proudly extends this legacy by serving as a strong bridge between the institution and its graduates.

United by shared values, experiences, and a deep sense of belonging, our alumni carry the spirit of UVOCCET into diverse fields across the globe. Guided by the vision of the college, the Association fosters lifelong connections, supports emerging students, and contributes to the continued growth and recognition of our alma mater.
