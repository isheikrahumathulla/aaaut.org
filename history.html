History & Milestones

Tracing the journey of our esteemed institution and the growth of our alumni community.

2008

College Established

University VOC College of Engineering was established as a constituent college of Anna University.

2009

First Batch Enrolled

The first batch of engineering students started their journey in Civil and Mechanical Engineering.

2013

Alumni Association Registered

The Alumni Association was officially formed and registered (Reg. 70/2013) to connect graduates.

2015

Campus Expansion

New academic blocks and laboratories were inaugurated to support the growing student strength.

2018

Decennial Celebrations

Celebrated 10 years of academic excellence with a grand alumni reunion.

2023

Grand Alumni Meet

A major gathering of alumni from all previous batches, strengthening the network.
